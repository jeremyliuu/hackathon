Drchrono Check-in Kiosk System ver.0.1.0-alpha

  • Tutorial:
    • Doctor can see appointments today after login.
    • Patients can provide their names(maybe SSN) in the check-in page.
    • After that, they need to select one appointment and update their information.
    • A page will be showed if they check-in successfully.
  • Additional Features:
    • Check-in Page button in Navbar. Open check-in page in new tab.
    • Appointment filter. Can show appointment list in three different status.
    • Auto-refreshing doctor page in 60 seconds. Refresh button allows you to refresh by hand.
    • Error message in check-in page if patients enter wrong info or have no appointments/already checked in.
    • Skip button in demographic info update page.
    • Redirect to check-in page after 'successfully check-in' message showed.
